Our Story

Fitness in Pink was founded in 2014. Only the Fitness class was available and was given in a small rental place and with only one coach and very few clients. Since then, the Fitness in Pink family has grown and now consists of three coaches and around 100 clients who have become members of our family. We do not rent anymore and are now proud owners of a building consisting of two exercise rooms and one swimming pool to conduct our activities.

What we do

We want to help you live a fit and healthy lifestyle! We do this by providing you with three different classes for you to choose from: Zumba, Fitness and Aqua Zumba. We want you to choose the activity most suitable to fulfill your mental and physical wellbeing. We reward all our clients for their hard work by organizing an End-Of-Year party which allows us to strengthen our bonds. Our team is always keen to help, so please call us.
